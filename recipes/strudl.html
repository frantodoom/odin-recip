Strudel

Description

Sweet baked cake filled with apples and cinnamon

Ingredients

  • Hazelnut Dough
  • Apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  1. Roll Hazelnut Dough
  2. Slice apples on slices and put them on dough
  3. To prevent dough from soaking juice from apples, sprinkle breadcrumbs on apples
  4. Sprinkle apples with cinnamon, and roll the dough into roll
  5. Spread eggs onto the roll and put the strudl into the oven
